Frequently Asked Questions

How do I login?

Use the email and password provided by your fleet manager. If you're a fleet manager, use your registered credentials.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Please contact your fleet administrator or email us at ahmedzayhn@gmail.com to reset your password.

How do I request a vehicle as a driver?

1. Login to the app
2. Tap "Request Vehicle" button on the dashboard
3. Select an available vehicle
4. Enter destination and reason
5. Submit request
6. Wait for master approval

How do I assign a vehicle as a master?

1. Login as fleet master
2. Tap on any available vehicle
3. Tap "Assign to Driver" button
4. Select a driver from the list
5. Enter destination
6. Confirm assignment

How do I start a trip?

Once a vehicle is assigned to you, you'll see a "Start Trip" button. Tap it when you begin your journey.

The app isn't loading. What should I do?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and reopen the app
3. If the issue persists, email us at ahmedzayhn@gmail.com

Can I view my trip history?

Yes! As a driver, you can view all your completed trips in the "Trip History" section.

Is my data secure?

Yes, FleetTracker uses Firebase for secure data storage with encryption in transit. All data is stored securely and only accessible by authorized users.

Privacy Policy

FleetTracker collects minimal user data necessary for app functionality:

  • Account Information: Email, name, phone number
  • Location Data: Vehicle location (optional, for tracking)
  • Trip Data: Trip history and assignments

We use Firebase for secure data storage. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Users can request data deletion by emailing ahmedzayhn@gmail.com.
